SECTION 1 — LISTING REQUIREMENTS
1.1 All exclusive right-to-sell listings must be entered into the MLS within 2 business days of the seller's signature.
1.2 Listing photos must be uploaded within 5 business days of entry. A minimum of 1 photo is required; the primary photo must depict the front exterior of the property.
1.3 Listings must include accurate square footage, bedroom count, bathroom count, and lot size.

SECTION 2 — STATUS CHANGES
2.1 Status changes (pending, sold, withdrawn, expired) must be reported within 2 business days of the event.
2.2 Sold prices must reflect the actual closing price as recorded on the settlement statement.

SECTION 3 — COMMISSIONS AND COOPERATION
3.1 Offers of compensation to cooperating brokers must be explicit and unconditional at the time of listing submission.
3.2 Changes to compensation may only be made in writing and communicated to all cooperating brokers before an offer is submitted.

SECTION 4 — VIOLATIONS AND FINES
4.1 First violation of any listing rule: written warning.
4.2 Second violation within 12 months: $100 fine.
4.3 Third and subsequent violations within 12 months: $250 fine per occurrence.
4.4 All fines must be paid within 30 days of notice. Unpaid fines may result in suspension of MLS access.

SECTION 5 — CODE OF CONDUCT
5.1 Members must treat all other members, clients, and customers with professionalism and respect.
5.2 Disparagement of competitors' listings or business practices in the MLS system is prohibited.
5.3 Members must comply with all applicable fair housing laws.
